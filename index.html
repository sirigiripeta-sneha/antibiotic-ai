Understanding the Crisis

The Growing Threat

Antibiotic resistance occurs when bacteria evolve to survive the drugs designed to kill them. It's one of the greatest threats to global health, turning once-treatable infections into potential death sentences.

Each year, 1.27 million deaths are directly attributed to antimicrobial resistance. Without intervention, this number could reach 10 million by 2050, surpassing cancer as a leading cause of death.

Our Approach

AI-Powered Solution

Hospital Data Analysis

Trend Forecasting
